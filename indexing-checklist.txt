Indexing checklist for Sahil Mungara portfolio
===========================================

1. Make sure the main site is live and publicly reachable.
2. Keep the sitemap updated at:
   https://sahilmungara.netlify.app/sitemap.xml
3. Ensure the homepage and new pages are linked from the site navigation.
4. Submit the homepage and important pages to Google Search Console.
5. Submit the sitemap to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
6. Share the site on social platforms and developer communities to increase crawl frequency.
7. Keep content updated regularly with fresh projects, achievements, and blog-like updates.
8. Use clear titles, descriptions, headings, and structured data for AI readability.
9. Confirm that pages return 200 OK and are not blocked by robots.txt.
10. Monitor indexing over the next few days to see whether new pages are discovered.
